About Me

Hi! I'm a queer fourth-year computer science student at the University of Waterloo with an interest in data management and web development. I have four work terms (16 months) worth of experience writing production-quality back-end and front-end code for custom web apps, and am excited to get some more experience under my belt -- in either the same or new fields entirely.

This term I'm taking both a security and networking course to further expand my knowledge within the field of web dev. I am also taking a course on how to write requirements for coding-related projects, to be able to better manage my time spent working.

When I'm not coding you will find me attempting to bake something new or reading the instruction manual for yet another euro game.
